Certificates, IDs & Profiles
As you may already know, a non-jailbroken iOS device is only able to run apps
approved by Apple and installed through the App Store.
Apple achieves this by requiring that every app run by iOS has a signed Apple
Certificate. Apps installed from the App Store come bundled with a certificate,
which the system verifies before it allows the app to run. If there’s no signature
or if the signature is invalid, the app won’t run.
As a developer, you need to be able to run your apps on your own devices on a
regular basis as you’re developing them. For this you need a way to create and
sign your own certificates.
